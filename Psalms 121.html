Psalms 121:2

Dear God, I thank you,
that my help comes from you.
-I will lift up mine,
eyes unto the hills,
from whence cometh my help.-
As Christians, we should never,
forget that God is the source,
of all of our help.
-My help comes from the *LORD.
He made the *heaven and the earth.-
He will send us people and things,
to help us but we shouldn't,
see the people as the source.
They are just God's vessels,
who are doing His work in our lives.
In times of distress,
no matter how overwhelmed,
I may feel, I know that,
I can trust in you, Lord.
You are my guide,
and my strength.
You keep me when,
the world tries to tear me apart.
In Jesus' name,
I pray.
Amen.
