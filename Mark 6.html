Mark 6:49-50

LORD, You make mighty miracles.
You do it with ease.
It is in Your nature to be miraculous.
- When they saw him walking upon the sea, -
-they supposed it had been a spirit, -
-and cried out: -
-For they all saw him, and were troubled.-
You make simple moments amazing.
You can walk on water.
You can move mountains.
-Immediately you talked with them, -
-and saith unto them, Be of good cheer: -
-it is I; be not afraid.-
Jesus is capable of doing anything.
Believe in His name,
and experience His greatness.
Thank You for Your might,
and Your goodness.
I love to hear of Your mighty ways.
In Jesus' name.
Amen.
