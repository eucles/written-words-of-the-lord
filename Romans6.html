Romans 6:17

Our hearts, O Lord,
were enslaved to sin,
unable to respond in love to you.
But now you have made,
us alive to righteousness.
-God be thanked, -
-that ye were the servants of sin, -
-but ye have obeyed,-
-from the heart,-
-that form of doctrine,-
-which has delivered you.-
Help us to continue,
to obey you from the heart,
and grow by the sanctifying power,
of your Word.
Only by obeying the Gospel,
from the heart can we be saved.
help me not to repeat praying,
or walking down an aisle,
at a religious event,
cause that does not save me.
Repent and believe!
is what saves us.
I thank God that I have become,
wholeheartedly obedient to the teachings,
which were given by the prophets.
Help us to strive after,
for the purpose of honoring,
you in all things.
I hear your voice, I will follow you, O Lord,
In your word, you give eternal life,
you promise I shall never perish.
In Jesus' name,
I pray,
Amen.
