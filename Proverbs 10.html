scripture prayers

Proverbs 10:7

Dear God, I pray that
I will always be kind
in my ways to everyone
around me.
-The memory of the righteous is blessed;-
People who strive
to be good and righteous
will always be
remembered with fondness.
I desire that when
I leave this earth,
I will be remembered
for being someone
who expressed your love
not someone who inflicted
pain on others.
the memory of people
who have lived their lives
hurting people,
will never be a good one.
-the name of the wicked shall rot.-
May I be known for being peaceful
and loving and always ready to help,
In Jesus' name,
I pray.
Amen.
