Godlike consciousness

It comes like a,
Rushing River-(acting or moving or capable of acting or moving quickly; swift).
Tumbling-(fall suddenly, clumsily, or headlong.)-
in flux-(process of flowing or flowing out.)
From above,
To the depths beneath,
The Manifest, (clear or obvious to the eye or mind.)
Moving and perishable,
Is governed,
By the unmanifest,(remaining hidden.)
Unmoving and imperishable ,
Consciousness is holy:
Uncorrupt and eternal,
And whatever,
Can be named;
Higher than that,
If" anything,
Can be!
Amen.
