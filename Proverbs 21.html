Proverbs 21:29

God you are my guide,
in all things,
grant me the courage of honesty.
-A wicked man hardeneth his face: -
Let me confess to my errors,
and seek to amend my ways.
-but as for the upright,
he directeth his way.-
The wicked or the weak.
will put up a front.
to cover their.
crimes or transgressions,
Behind the sweetest of smiles.
may lurk the most devastating characters.
Let me not hide or deceive others,
but act in accordance with your Law.
God knows the truth,
even if people may be fooled.
For I trust in your mercy Lord,
and know that justice is yours,
Amen.
