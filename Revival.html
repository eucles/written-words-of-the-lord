Revival

"Dear Father, Please forgive,
the society I live in.
Father, I live in a world,
of increasing postmodern thought,
and self-love.
Please bring a revival to my area,
that people would repent,
of the things they do,
that displease you,
and have their heart turned to you.
Help me take a part in that revival,
to speak when I should speak,
and to stand up,
when others are tempted to sit back.
I am thankful that you can move
in even the darkest of places.
Give me the eyes to see You.
In Jesus's name,
I pray,
Amen.
