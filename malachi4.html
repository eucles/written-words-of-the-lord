Malachi 4:1

1 For, behold, the day cometh,
that shall burn as an oven;
and all the proud, yea,
and all that do wickedly,
shall be stubble:
and the day that cometh,
shall burn them up,
saith the LORD of hosts,
that it shall leave,
them neither root nor branch.
2 But unto you,
that fear my name,
shall the Son of righteousness,
arise with healing in his wings;
and ye shall go forth,
and grow up as calves of the stall.
3 And ye shall tread,
down the wicked;
for they shall be ashes,
under the soles of your feet,
in the day that I shall do this,
]saith the LORD of hosts.
