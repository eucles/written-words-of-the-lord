2 chronicles 6:18

O Lord, you are the omni-
You are everywhere-
present God.
heaven and the heaven of heavens,
cannot contain thee;
how much less,
this house which I have built!
no not the entirety,
of the created universe.
The universe is vast,
beyond our ability,
to comprehend,
yet as a created thing,
we know it must have its limits.
But you are infinite.
Your divine nature is beyond,
our ability to comprehend,
and we feel our smallness.
we also know,
you dwell in us,
your people,
and hold intimate communion,
with our spirits every day.
In Jesus'name.
I pray.
Amen.
